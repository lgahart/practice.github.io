"Project One" Digital Video, 3 min, 2019

This first project is of a day spent in San Fransisco with my roommates exploring the city. The concept behind this video is to show the contant video documentation of our lives through instagram/snapchat stories and youtube vlogs.

"Project Two" Digital Video, 2:11 mins, 2019

This project was displayed through a video projection mapped on a canvas. This canvas is surrounded by bottles of alcohol and candles to represent a shrine. The message behind this project is to show how we often battle with addictions and finding the peace within.

"Project Three" Net Art, 2019

This project shows interviews by three different women and their experiences with sexiest comments and how they have effected them and how they perceived their bodies.

"Final Project" Digital Video, 3:36 mins, 2019

This final project is meant to represent the sudden rise of facetune and how destructive it is to girl's body image. Many different youtubers and vlogers have touched on this topic and those clips are shown in the beggining of the video. The video ends with a shot of dolls surrounded by makeup and other items that girls use to change how they look.
